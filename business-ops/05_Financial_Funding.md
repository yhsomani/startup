## Indian Tax System

### Corporate Tax

**Section 115BAA** ‚≠ê (Recommended)
- Rate: 22% + 10% surcharge + 4% cess
- **Effective: 25.17%**
- No exemptions/deductions

**Normal Regime:**
- 30% + surcharge + cess = 32-35%

**Manufacturing (115BAB):**
- 15% + cess = 17.16%

### GST (18% for most IT/SaaS)

**Registration:**
- Mandatory: >‚Çπ20L turnover (services)
- Or inter-state sales

**Returns:**
- **GSTR-1:** 11th (outward supplies)
- **GSTR-3B:** 20th (summary + payment)
- **GSTR-9:** Dec 31 (annual)

### TDS Rates

| Payment | Section | Rate | Threshold |
|---------|---------|------|-----------|
| Professional Fee | 194J | 10% | ‚Çπ30K/year |
| Rent | 194I | 10% | ‚Çπ2.4L/year |
| Contractor | 194C | 1-2% | ‚Çπ30K single |
| Salary | 192 | Slab | All |

**Deposit:** 7th of next month  
**Return:** Quarterly (31st July/Oct/Jan/Apr)
