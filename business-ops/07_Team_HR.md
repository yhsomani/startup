## EPF & ESI (Statutory)

### EPF (Employees Provident Fund)

**When Mandatory:** 20+ employees (10+ in some states)

**Contribution:**
- Employee: 12% of basic
- Employer: 12% of basic (3.67% EPF + 8.33% EPS)

**Example:**

## ESOP (India)

### Tax Treatment (DPIIT Startups)

**Benefit:** Tax deferred till:
- Sale of shares, OR
- 5 years from grant, OR
- Resignation
(Whichever is earliest)

**Must be:** DPIIT-recognized startup

**Normal Taxation (Non-DPIIT):**
- Tax at exercise as perquisite
- Tax at sale as capital gains

---

### Typical Structure

**Pool:** 10-15% of shares

**Vesting:** 4 years, 1-year cliff  
(25% after year 1, then quarterly)

**Exercise Price:** FMV or â‚¹10/share

**Grant Letter:**
```
Dear [Employee],

You are granted [X] stock options.

Exercise Price: â‚¹[Y] per share
Vesting: 25% after 12 months, then quarterly
Exercise: Within 5 years of vesting

Subject to ESOP-2024 scheme.

For Company:             Accepted:
___________              ___________
```
