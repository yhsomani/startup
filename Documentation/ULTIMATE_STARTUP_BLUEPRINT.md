## 1. STARTUP FUNDAMENTALS

### What is a Startup
A company designed for rapid growth, scalable business model, innovative solution, typically tech-enabled, seeking product-market fit in 0-3 years.

### Startup Types
**SaaS:** Subscription software (Zoho, Salesforce) | **Marketplace:** Connect buyers-sellers (Uber, Airbnb) | **E-commerce:** Online retail (Amazon, Flipkart) | **FinTech:** Financial services (Stripe, Razorpay) | **D2C:** Direct consumer brands (Warby Parker) | **B2B:** Enterprise solutions (Slack, Salesforce)

## 2. PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION & VALIDATION

### Problem Discovery Framework
**Criteria:** (1) Affects 100K+ people globally (2) Current solutions cost $50-500/month (3) You have unique insight/advantage (4) Tech-solvable (5) Regulatory-favorable

**Sources:** Personal pain point | Industry experience | Market gaps | Technology enablement | Regulatory changes

### Validation Process (8 Weeks)

**Weeks 1-2: Customer Discovery**
- Identify 100 potential customers (LinkedIn, Reddit, communities)
- Conduct 50+ interviews (30-min each)
- Script: "What's your biggest challenge with [problem]?" ‚Üí "How do you solve it today?" ‚Üí "Would you pay $X monthly for a solution?"
- **Success:** 60%+ say "yes, I'd pay"

**Weeks 3-4: Market Research**
- Calculate TAM/SAM/SOM (Total/Serviceable/Obtainable Market in $ and ‚Çπ)
- Example: Project Management SaaS
  - Global TAM: $50B | SAM: $5B (SMBs) | Year 1 SOM: $175K
  - India TAM: ‚Çπ2,000Cr | SAM: ‚Çπ500Cr | Year 1 SOM: ‚Çπ1.45Cr
- Identify 10 competitors (3 direct, 7 indirect)
- SWOT analysis: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats

**Weeks 5-6: MVP Planning**
- Define: Minimum Viable Product (simplest version that delivers core value)
- MVP Types: Landing page ($500, 3 days) | Figma prototype ($2K, 1 week) | No-code (Bubble/Webflow, $5K, 2 weeks) | Coded MVP ($20K, 3 months)
- Choose based on complexity and budget

**Weeks 7-8: Beta Testing**
- Recruit 20-50 beta users (from interviews)
- Deliver MVP, gather feedback
- **Success Metrics:** 50% activation | 30% weekly retention | NPS >40

**Validation Checkpoint:** If <40% customers willing to pay OR <30% beta retention ‚Üí Pivot or abandon

## 5. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

### India Company Registration (Recommended: Private Limited)

**Why Pvt Ltd:** Limited liability | VC-fundable | Tax benefits | Separate legal entity | Can have foreign shareholders

**Requirements:** 2 Directors (Indian residents, 18+) | 2 Shareholders (can overlap) | 1 Registered address | ‚Çπ1L authorized capital

**Registration Process (10-14 days, ‚Çπ10-20K):**

**Day 1-2:** Name Reservation
- Check availability: mca.gov.in
- File RUN (Reserve Unique Name) form
- Cost: ‚Çπ1,000

**Day 3-5:** Get DSC & DIN
- DSC (Digital Signature): ‚Çπ1,000-2,000/director via eMudhra/Sify
- DIN (Director ID): Auto-generated with SPICe+ form

**Day 6-10:** File SPICe+ (Integrated Form)
- **Documents:** Directors' PAN, Aadhaar, photo, address proof | Office rental agreement + NOC OR ownership deed | MOA (objectives) | AOA (internal rules)
- **Fees:** Government ‚Çπ500-3,000 + Professional ‚Çπ8-15K = Total ‚Çπ10-20K
- **Included:** CIN, PAN, TAN, GSTIN (if opted), EPFO, ESIC registrations

**Day 11-14:** Post-Incorporation
- **Receive:** Certificate of Incorporation (COI) + CIN, Company PAN, TAN
- **Action:** Open current account (ICICI/HDFC recommended) | Deposit capital ‚Çπ10K-1L | Apply DPIIT Startup India recognition (FREE, online, 2-7 days)

**Critical Legal Documents:**
1. **Founder Agreement:** Equity split, vesting (4 years, 1-year cliff), IP assignment, roles, exit terms
2. **Shareholders Agreement:** If >2 founders, investor rights later
3. **Employment Agreements:** For all team members (IP ownership critical)
4. **NDA/Confidentiality:** For partners, contractors
5. **Terms of Service & Privacy Policy:** For website/app (GDPR-compliant if global)

**Annual Compliance:**
- AOC-4 (Financial statements): Within 30 days of AGM
- MGT-7 (Annual return): Within 60 days of AGM
- AGM (Annual General Meeting): By Sept 30
- DIR-3 KYC (Directors): By Sept 30 annually
- ITR (Income Tax Return): By Oct 31 (if audit) or July 31
- Statutory Audit: Mandatory for Pvt Ltd

## 6. TAX & FINANCIAL COMPLIANCE (INDIA)

### Corporate Income Tax
**Rate:** 25.17% effective (Section 115BAA - new regime, recommended) | Alternative: 30%+surcharge old regime

**Startup Benefit (DPIIT-recognized):**
- **Tax Holiday:** 100% income tax exemption for 3 consecutive years (out of first 10) if approved by Inter-Ministerial Board
- **Eligibility:** Turnover <‚Çπ100Cr, innovative, DPIIT-recognized

**Filing:** ITR-6 by Oct 31 (if audit required) or July 31

### GST (Goods & Services Tax)
**Registration:** Mandatory if turnover >‚Çπ20L (services) or ‚Çπ40L (goods) | Inter-state sales: Mandatory from Day 1

**Rate:** 18% for IT/SaaS services (most common)

**Export Exemption:** 0% GST on services to international customers (with invoice, FIRC proof)

**Returns:**
- **GSTR-1:** 11th of next month (outward supplies)
- **GSTR-3B:** 20th + payment (summary return)
- **GSTR-9:** Dec 31 (annual return)

**Late Fee:** ‚Çπ200/day (‚Çπ100 CGST + ‚Çπ100 SGST)

### TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)
| Payment Type | Section | Rate | Threshold |
|--------------|---------|------|-----------|
| Salary | 192 | Slab rates | All employees |
| Professional fees (CA, lawyer) | 194J | 10% | >‚Çπ30K/year |
| Rent | 194I | 10% | >‚Çπ2.4L/year |
| Contractor/Freelancer | 194C | 1-2% | >‚Çπ30K single, ‚Çπ1L annual |

**Deposit:** By 7th of next month | **Return:** Quarterly (24Q for salary, 26Q for others, due 31st July/Oct/Jan/Apr)

### Payroll Taxes (if hiring employees)

**EPF (Employees' Provident Fund) - If 20+ employees:**
- Employee: 12% of basic salary
- Employer: 12% (3.67% EPF + 8.33% EPS + 0.5% admin)
- Payment: 15th of month
- Registration: epfindia.gov.in

**ESI (Employees' State Insurance) - If 10+ employees, earning <‚Çπ21K/month:**
- Employee: 0.75% of gross
- Employer: 3.25% of gross
- Payment: 15th of month

**Professional Tax:** State-specific (Maharashtra/Karnataka: ‚Çπ200/month for salary >‚Çπ10-15K)

### Compliance Calendar
**Monthly:**
- 7th: TDS deposit
- 11th: GSTR-1 filing
- 15th: EPF, ESI payment, Professional Tax
- 20th: GSTR-3B + GST payment

**Quarterly:**
- 15th (Jun/Sep/Dec/Mar): Advance tax payment (if profit)
- 31st (Jul/Oct/Jan/Apr): TDS returns (24Q, 26Q)

**Annually:**
- June 15: Form 16 to employees
- July 31: ITR (if no audit)
- Sept 30: AGM, DIR-3 KYC
- Oct 31: AOC-4, MGT-7, ITR (if audit)
- Dec 31: GSTR-9

## 16. GLOSSARY

**ARR:** Annual Recurring Revenue (MRR √ó 12)  
**CAC:** Customer Acquisition Cost ((Sales + Marketing) / New customers)  
**Churn:** % customers who cancel monthly  
**CIN:** Corporate Identification Number (India company ID)  
**DPIIT:** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (Startup India)  
**ESOP:** Employee Stock Option Plan (equity for team)  
**GDPR:** General Data Protection Regulation (EU privacy law)  
**GST:** Goods & Services Tax (India VAT, typically 18%)  
**LTV:** Lifetime Value (Revenue per customer over lifetime)  
**MRR:** Monthly Recurring Revenue  
**MVP:** Minimum Viable Product (simplest version to test)  
**NPS:** Net Promoter Score (customer satisfaction, -100 to 100)  
**PMF:** Product-Market Fit (customers love and retain product)  
**PRD:** Product Requirements Document  
**SAM:** Serviceable Available Market (subset of TAM you can serve)  
**SAFE:** Simple Agreement for Future Equity (fundraising instrument)  
**SOM:** Serviceable Obtainable Market (realistic capture in 1-3 years)  
**TAM:** Total Addressable Market (total market size)  
**TDS:** Tax Deducted at Source (India withholding tax)
